Controlling Video with JavaScript

Video is easy to control with JavaScript. You can use the default controls on the <video> tag or create your own controls to match the branding on your website. To control the size of the video we just set a value for the video's width using CSS.

The <video> tag does support autoplay but most browsers have opted out of the feature and instead will try to evaluate the trustworthiness of your site before allowing autoplay to function.
